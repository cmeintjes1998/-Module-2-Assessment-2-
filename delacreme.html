DE LA CREME

De La Crème or Crème de la crème means or is an idiom(In French) which means the best of the best or the very best. We are a bakery, café and deli which had been around for 33 years we were established in 1987. We are a family owned and run business that has been providing all these years the freshest and purest ingredients, food, pastry and sweets to all our customers. Fresh croissants, breads, beautiful breakfast and lunches come out of our bakery, kitchen and deli on a daily basis.
